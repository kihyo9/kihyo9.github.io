Overwatch

Tanks

Tanks are capable of taking lots of damage, protecting teammates, and initiating fights. They often have powerful CC abilities (Roadhog's hook, Reinhardt's shatter etc.) and can deal large amounts of deal in certain situations. Sub-classification include off-tanks and main tanks.

Damage/Dealers/DPS

DPS heroes can inflict lots of damage. Their high damage output, or the threat of it, applies pressure on the enemy team that allows the DPS heroes' team to contest and overtake an objective. Sub-classification include hitscan, flankers, snipers, burst, and builders.

Supports/Healers

Support heroes sustain their team's abilities to fight. Their most important capability is health restoration. They carry various useful utilities, such as damage amplification, disables, and immunity/reduction to damage taken. Sub-classification include off-healers, and main healers.
